Seven Layered Bean Dip

Ingredients: -CHEESE (SHREDDED) -GUACAMOLE (STORE BOUGHT OR HOMEMADE) -LETTUCE -LIME - 2 TBSP MAYO -BLACK OLIVES -ONION POWDER -REFRIED BEANS -SOUR CREAM -TACO SEASONING -TOMATOES

Instructions: Layer the following ingredients in an clear 9 X 13 baking dish. First layer, mix 1/3 of the taco seasoning with the refried beans. The beans are the first layer. Second layer, mix onion powder, the mayo, and lime into the guacamole. Spread the guacamole on top of the beans. Third layer, mix the remaining taco seasoning with the sour cream. Spread the sour cream on top of the guacamole. Fourth layer, spread out the chopped lettuce on top of the sour cream. Fifth layer, spread out the chopped tomatoes on top of the lettuce. Sixth layer, spread the shredded cheddar cheese on top of the tomatoes. Seventh layer (the last layer), spread out the black olives on top of the cheese.

Keep in the refridgerator to stay fresh. Serve with tortilla chips or whatever dipping alternative.
